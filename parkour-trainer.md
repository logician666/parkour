# Parkour Conditioning — Programme Overview

> A 16-week periodised conditioning programme designed for home training with minimal equipment. Phases are gated — each unlocks only when the prior phase criteria are met.

[[_hub|→ Go to Hub]]

---

## Programme architecture

| Phase | Name | Weeks | Goal |
|-------|------|-------|------|
| 1 | [[phase-1-foundation\|Foundation]] | 1–2 | Joint prep, baseline mobility, deep stabilisers |
| 2 | [[phase-2-static-strength\|Static Strength]] | 3–4 | Isometric endurance, push strength, wall support |
| 3 | [[phase-3-dynamic-strength\|Dynamic Strength]] | 5–6 | Full ROM strength, explosive hip extension |
| 4 | [[phase-4-explosive-power\|Explosive Power]] | 7–8 | Nervous system — rapid force production |
| 5 | [[phase-5-body-control\|Body Control]] | 9–12 | Balance, precision, quadrupedal flow, spatial awareness |
| 6 | [[phase-6-integration\|Pre-Parkour Integration]] | 13–16 | Flowing circuits — readiness assessment |

---

## Phase unlock criteria

| Transition | Criteria |
|-----------|---------|
| Phase 1 → 2 | All 6 sessions complete (3 sessions × 2 weeks) |
| Phase 2 → 3 | 15 push-ups in one set + wall sit 60s |
| Phase 3 → 4 | Nordic curl 80% depth · stick landing quality 4/5 · dead hang 40s |
| Phase 4 → 5 | Silent depth drop · explosive push-up hands airborne · reactive circuit quality stable |
| Phase 5 → 6 | Full Phase 5 assessment (see [[phase-5-body-control]]) |

---

## Equipment required

- Door frame with accessible top bar for hanging
- Finger extension band
- Grip strengthener
- Unobstructed wall with floor space in front
- Mat or carpet (Phase 5 rolls onwards)

---

## Logging

Sessions are logged as notes using the **Templater** session template. Each log note links back to its phase note, building a navigable graph of your training history. Progress is aggregated in [[_hub|the Hub]] via Dataview.

Log fields used across all sessions:
- `feel` (1–5) — overall session quality
- `key_result` — the standout number or quality score for the day

Benchmark results (used to unlock phases) are logged in the exercise table within each session note.
